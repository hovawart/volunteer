A volunteer listening network

Someone to talk to, no appointment needed to matter.

Porchlight connects you with a trained volunteer who will simply listen — no diagnoses, no advice you didn't ask for, no judgment. Just a real conversation, on your terms.

Request a session Become a listener
Porchlight is peer support, not therapy or crisis care. If you're in danger or in crisis, call or text 988 (US) or your local emergency number.
How it works

Three steps, start to finish

01 / Request

Tell us a little

Fill out a short form with what's on your mind and when you're free. It takes about two minutes, and you can say as little or as much as you like.

02 / Match

We find a listener

A volunteer who fits your availability reaches out to confirm a time. You'll always know who you're talking to before the conversation starts.

03 / Talk

Have the conversation

By phone, video, or chat — whatever feels easiest. Sessions are unhurried, confidential, and led by what you want to talk about.

Clear expectations

What Porchlight is — and isn't

It is

  • A free, informal space to talk something through
  • Volunteers trained in active listening and boundaries
  • Confidential, judgment-free, and led by you
  • A companion to — not a replacement for — your own support system

It isn't

  • Licensed therapy, counseling, or medical advice
  • A crisis or emergency line — see our footer for those resources
  • Able to guarantee a specific listener or response time
  • A place for diagnoses, prescriptions, or treatment plans
